Skylar Yarter

Computer Science Student at Williams College

As a computer science major at Williams College in Massachusetts' beautiful Berkshire mountains, I have had the opportunity to explore a breadth of computer science topics in my classes while embracing a true liberal arts education.

My computer science courses have ranged from covering the fundamentals, like algorithms and computer organization, to more specialized areas, like robotics and human-computer interaction.

Outside of my major, I've taken classes like "Thinking the Family Album," "The Nature of Nature," and "The Ethics of AI." I've gotten to explore comparative literature, philosophy, sociology, psychology, and more in my time at Williams.

Outside of the classroom, being in the Berkshires has given me countless opportunities to get outside and build close relationships with the people around me. I am the president of our women's ultimate frisbee team, Nova, and in the summers, I lead hiking trips around the world for middle- and high-schoolers!

I am a senior at Williams and originally from New Jersey. After college, I plan on moving to Boston.
