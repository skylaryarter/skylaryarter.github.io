Reimagining Study Away for Williams Seniors

Project Date March 6th, 2025
Read More Full write-up

For this design sprint, my group was assigned Williams seniors as our user group, and decided to redesign the Williams Study Away website for mobile phones using the prototyping website Figma.

We focused on making our redesign intuitive to use, visually appealing, and visually consistent with the Williams College brand identity. Additionally, in order to center our designated user group, we prioritized information and design choices most relevant to seniors.
