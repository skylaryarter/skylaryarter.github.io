The TikTok Experience for Blind and Low Vision Users

Project Date May 1st, 2025
Read More Full write-up

In this design sprint, I was tasked with evaluating the accessibility of the TikTok app for users with visual impairments.

I familiarized myself with accessibility standards, and then spent time collecting first-hand accounts of blind and low vision TikTok users. This allowed me to identify some accessibility issues with TikTok's design and recommend improvements.
