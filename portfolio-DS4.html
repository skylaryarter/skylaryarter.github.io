Judging a Book by Its Catalog: Redesigning with Users in Mind

Project Date April 17th, 2025
Read More Full write-up

Starting with a basic book catalog website, we iteratively redesigned the site to be more functional and visually appealing, and then conducted usability evaluations on the redesign.

We planned a multi-part evaluation that included interviews, site walk throughs, and a survey in order to collect both qualitative and quantitative data about the usability of our design.
